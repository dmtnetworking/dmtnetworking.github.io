The Information & Computer Technology major, there are three areas of concentration. The three areas are: Networking, Security, and Information Technology. Each one allows the student to specialize in their desired career field. IT is a broad field, so specialization is key, and East Carolina University is happy to support it's students ability to be exposed to inter-discipline areas of studies.

Each Information & Computer Technology major is required to complete their core classes these classes are meant to make each student well-rounded and exposed to a variety of technologies. These classes include an internship, and a two-semester long capstone project. The requirements are as follows:
