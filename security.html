Security Concentration

The IT Security concentration is a highly-respected concentration for students to persue. This concentration allows students to become a threat to hackers, and anyone malicious towards the students employers. Students will cover course work that involves honeypots, detection of breaches, enterprise management, and ways to ensure that information is held securely.

There is currently a massive world-wide shortage of Cybersecuriy experts. Due to this, the demand is high, and so is the pay. Students are regularly visited by employers, such as the NSA, who recruit directly from the concentration.
