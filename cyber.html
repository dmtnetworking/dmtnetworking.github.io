New Cyberdefense Room

ECU recently invested $500k into a new cyber-defense room that is designed to provide a center of resources for students. Used a classroom for various classes, including the Capstone Project, it features four tables, each with a TV attached to it's base. These tables have rise-up dual-monitors for each PC. In addition, there is a 5 TV x 5 TV wall display. There are also microphone and cameras for competitions and lectures.
