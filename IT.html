The IT concentration for the ICTN degree is a generalized concentration that aims to make its students well-rounded. It includes some courses from the other concentrations as well, such as ICTN 4064 Regulations and Policies. While this concentration is not specialized, it does have it's benefits for students who don't know which branch of IT they wish to persue.
