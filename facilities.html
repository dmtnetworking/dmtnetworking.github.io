Facilities

Science & Technology Building

  • New Cyberdefense Room

The College of Engineering & Technology is located in Science and Technology building, SciTech. SciTech is a newer building on campus, and it remains the largest central campus building. It is so large that it is shared between multiple departments, and includes Computer Science and Chemistry. This building hosts hands-on Cisco labs, hands-on industrial production facilities, and is home to events throughout the year. Cisco, NetApp, and RedHat all make regular visits to this building.
