Department of Technology Systems

About

The Information & Computer Technology is apart Technology Systems department within the College of Engineering. Dr. Tijjani Mohammed is the department chair, and he oversees the operations of the department. ICTN is not the only major in the department, there is also:

  • BS in Design

  • BS in Industrial Distribution & Logistics

  • BS in Industrial Engineering Technology

  • BS in Industrial Technology Transfer Program

  • BS in Information & Computer Technology

This website covers degree requirements, the programs facilities and resources, courses, and the wonderful faculty that keep the gears turning. If you have any questions, please go to the "Contact Us" tab in the top right corner of the web page. We will be more than happy to assist you!
