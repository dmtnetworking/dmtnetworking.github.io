Information & Computer Technology

Resources

The resources that ECU provides to its students is one of the strongest parts of the ICTN degree. ICTN majors have access to a hands-on cisco labs with lab monitors on-staff. Additional RedHat labs are available. These resources assist students who go for industry recognized certifications, which students are provided a discount for.
