Security Concentration

ECU's networking concentration prepares students for the CCNP certification. All students of the concentration must possess a current CCNA certification to begin the coursework. When students complete the concentration with their CCNP, they are ready to enter an industry where CCNPs are paid an average of $109,564 a year.

ITCN 3250/1 Internetwork Routing Technology & Lab
ITCN 4150/1 Switching Network Technology & Lab
ITCN 4250/1 Enterprise Network Security Technology & Lab
