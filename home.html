Information & Computer Technology

Welcome!

This is the home page for the College of Engineering's major of Information & Computer Technology. Dubbed ITCN, this major is preparing the next-generation of Network Engineers, Systems Administators, and more. This major is a 4-year program with an extensive record proving it's worth in the industry. Graduates regularly go on to large companies such as Red Hat, Cisco, NetApp, and more.

This website covers degree requirements, the programs facilities and resources, courses, and the wonderful faculty that keep the gears turning. If you have any questions, please go to the "Contact Us" tab in the top right corner of the web page. We will be more than happy to assist you!
