Cisco Day

On the day before the CET Career Fair, Cisco visits the Science & Technology building. Their booth allows students to network with the networking company, and Cisco offers several small events. They offer mock interviews, which helps students prepare. In addition, the come to campus to recruit for the Cisco co-op.
